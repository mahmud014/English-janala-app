Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, the website is free to use. You can access lessons, exercises, and quizzes without payment. (Some platforms may offer optional premium features, but the core learning content is usually free.)
3. Do I need to create an account?
In most cases, you can browse some lessons without an account, but creating one lets you track your progress, save exercises, and access all features.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can improve your vocabulary by reading regularly, practicing with flashcards, using new words in sentences, and trying interactive exercises or quizzes. Watching movies, listening to podcasts, and speaking with others also helps strengthen vocabulary.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes, many platforms provide certificates once you successfully complete a course. These certificates can be downloaded, shared, and sometimes even added to your LinkedIn profile to showcase your achievement.
